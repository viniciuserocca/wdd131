What is SAP?

SAP, or "Systems, Applications, and Products in Data Processing," is a global leader in enterprise resource planning (ERP) software. Founded in 1972, SAP provides solutions for managing core business functions, such as finance, human resources, and supply chain operations. Its flagship product, S/4HANA, enables real-time data analysis and streamlines complex processes, improving efficiency and collaboration. SAP supports cloud, AI, and analytics, aiding digital transformation across various industries.

What's inside the SD module in SAP?

The SAP Sales and Distribution (SD) module is designed to manage sales processes and customer distribution. It includes functions for handling customer orders, pricing, billing, shipping, and delivery. Key components within SD are Sales Order Processing, Pricing Management, Billing and Invoicing, and Credit Management. It integrates seamlessly with other SAP modules, like MM (Materials Management) and FI (Financial Accounting), enhancing data flow and operational efficiency.
